Product Overview

POCO M6

The POCO M6 delivers aflagship-like experience at an affordable price, featuring a stunning 6.79″ FHD+ 90 Hz display, a powerful 108 MP AI camera with 3× in-sensor zoom, and the efficient Helio G91-Ultra processor for smooth performance. With up to 16 GB RAM (with extension), a long-lasting 5030 mAh battery, and 33 W fast charging, it’s built for both power and endurance. Wrapped in a sleek glass design with modern essentials like a side fingerprint sensor, IR blaster, and headphone jack, the POCO M6 perfectly balances style, performance, and value.
